## Risk Assessment

### High Risk
1. **Appwrite Function Integration** - External service dependency
   - Mitigation: Mock implementations for testing

2. **Health Connect/HealthKit** - Platform-specific quirks
   - Mitigation: Extensive testing on target devices

3. **SQLCipher Encryption** - Key management complexity
   - Mitigation: Use platform keychain, thorough testing

### Medium Risk
4. **Sync Conflict Resolution** - Data consistency
   - Mitigation: Last-write-wins with timestamps

5. **Bilingual Rendering** - Hindi font rendering issues
   - Mitigation: Use system Noto Sans Devanagari

### Low Risk
6. **Lottie Animations** - Asset loading
   - Mitigation: Pre-load critical animations
